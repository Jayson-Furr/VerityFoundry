# Expected Workspace Outline

Candidate VeritySpec workspace areas:

- Product record for the customer portal.
- User and audience records for account owners, billing contacts, support
  admins, and internal support staff.
- Feature records for account status, user management, invoice download,
  support requests, and service-health review.
- Integration assumption records for billing, support, identity, and service
  health systems.
- Permission and audit decision records with human approval required.
- Readiness gap records for design, implementation, operations, privacy,
  accessibility, maintenance, and archive unknowns.

This outline is a candidate design-complete draft. It does not certify the
portal as implementation-ready or operations-ready.
