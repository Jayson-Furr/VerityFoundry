# Expected Assumptions

- The first target is a business customer portal.
- Account owners, billing contacts, support admins, and internal support staff
  are candidate user groups from the brief.
- Billing, support, identity, and service-health integrations are likely but
  unresolved.
- Permissions and audit logging require human approval before high-stakes
  claims can be made.
- Accessibility and privacy requirements must remain unresolved until reviewed.
