# Customer Portal Image Notes

No binary images are included in this example. These notes model how a product
team might describe early visual inputs for a customer portal workspace draft.

## Dashboard Wireframe

- A rough dashboard wireframe shows account status, billing summary, service
  health, and open support requests in one operational view.
- The wireframe implies a role-aware layout, but it does not prove permission
  behavior, audit retention, accessibility compliance, or production UI scope.

## Style Board

- A lightweight style board suggests a restrained business-product visual
  direction with neutral surfaces, clear status colors, and dense but readable
  information hierarchy.
- Brand approval, color accessibility, icon usage, and commercial clearance
  remain unknown.
