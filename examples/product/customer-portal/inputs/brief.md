# Customer Portal Brief

The customer portal lets business customers view account status, manage users,
download invoices, create support requests, and review service health.

Initial users are account owners, billing contacts, support admins, and
internal support staff. The first draft should focus on product intent and
design completeness, not implementation readiness.

Open concerns include permissions, billing-system integration, support-system
integration, audit logging, accessibility, privacy, data retention, and
operational support.
